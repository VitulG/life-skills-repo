#Focus Management

# Deep Work
It means working with full concentration on one important task without interruptions like phone notifications, social media, multitasking, random switching between tasks. Your mind enters a state of deep concentration where you think clearly and creatively.

# how to do deep work properly?
1. Choose one important task only
		i. No multitasking.
		ii. High Value.
		
2. Remove all distractions before starting
		i. Phone on silent.
		ii. Close Social media apps.
	
3. Set a fixed time block
		i. Means next 60 minutes do only this task.
		ii. No breaks no switching.
		
4. Use a tight environment
		i. Laptops open only for code or study
		ii. Use earphones if needed.
		
# How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?
1. Plan Your Day Around Deep Work Hours.
2. Identify Your Highest-Value Task.
3. Eliminate All Distractions Before Starting.
4. Time-Block Your Focus Sessions.
5. No Multitasking in between.
6. Define a Clear Goal for Each Session.
7. Stop at the Right Time.
8. End the Day With a Shutdown Ritual.

# What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
1. Destroys deep focus and makes your mind addicted to constant interruptions.
2. Reduces productivity by pulling you into short, shallow tasks.
3. Rewires your brain to crave likes, notifications, and instant rewards.
4. Increases anxiety and creates fear of missing out (FOMO).
5. Trains you to be distracted rather than thoughtful or creative.
6. Consumes free time that could be used for meaningful learning or skill-building.
7. Weakens your ability to do deep work, which is essential in modern careers.
